About Us

Vishwa Shuryam is a student-led club at Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology (VIIT), Pune, dedicated to nurturing and guiding defence aspirants. Our mission is to inspire, prepare, and support students aiming for a career in the Indian Armed Forces and other defence services. Since our inception, we have provided a platform for holistic development, leadership training, and awareness about opportunities in the defence sector.

Our Journey

Founded by a group of passionate students, Vishwa Shuryam has grown into a vibrant community for defence aspirants at VIIT. The club organizes regular seminars, workshops, and interactive sessions with defence personnel to provide insights into the life and challenges of the armed forces. We are committed to fostering a spirit of patriotism, discipline, and service among our members.

Over the years, Vishwa Shuryam has become a trusted resource for students preparing for various defence entrance exams, including NDA, CDS, AFCAT, and SSB interviews. Our journey is marked by the success of our members who have gone on to serve the nation with pride and honor.
