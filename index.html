Our Vision

To inspire and prepare students for a career in the Indian Armed Forces by fostering discipline, leadership, and patriotism. We strive to build a community that values service, integrity, and personal growth through training, mentorship, and teamwork.

  • Participate in defense-related workshops and training sessions
  • Connect with like-minded peers and experienced mentors
